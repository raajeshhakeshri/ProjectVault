RCA Matrix

5W1H & 5-Why Root Cause Diagnostics

Phase 1: Problem Framing

Context Explorer (4W)
WHAT W1

Clearly define the exact deviation, defect, or failure point. Avoid assumptions.

WHERE W2

Specify the physical, geographical, or systematic location of the failure.

WHEN W3

Establish a timeline. Include exact timestamps, duration, and operational phase.

WHO W4

Identify who discovered it and which user groups or stakeholders are impacted.

Impact Analyzer (1H)
HOW H1

Quantify how this issue is manifesting. Focus on the scale of the magnitude, trend lines, operational disruptions, and calculated financial or business cost.

Phase 2: Deep Drill-Down

WHY 1: The Symptom Level 1

Based on your 4W1H data, why did the immediate technical or physical failure occur?

WHY 2: The Excuse Level 2

Why did the situation described in Level 1 happen? Look at immediate process gaps.

WHY 3: The Process Level 3

Why was this process gap or code error allowed to proceed?

WHY 4: The System Level 4

Why did the organizational system allow the SOP to fail or be bypassed?

WHY 5: The Root Cause Root

What is the fundamental systemic, cultural, or policy breakdown that guarantees this failure will repeat unless structurally fixed?
